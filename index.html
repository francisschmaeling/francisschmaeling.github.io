About Me

I am an English and ESL educator with over 8 years of experience teaching in high school and adult education settings. I specialize in creating engaging, inclusive learning environments that foster language development and critical thinking skills. I'm committed to culturally responsive teaching and continuous professional growth, ensuring that all students feel valued and supported. My goal is to empower students to become confident communicators and lifelong learners.
