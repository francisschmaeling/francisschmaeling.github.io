Lesson Plan

Below is a condensed outline of a lesson plan I developed for an intermediate ESL class to enhance reading comprehension skills.

  • Topic: Reading Comprehension (Short Story)
  • Level: Intermediate ESL
  • Duration: 45 minutes
  • Objective: Improve students' ability to identify main ideas and details in a text.

Lesson Outline

  1. Warm-up: Begin with a quick discussion about students' favorite stories to activate prior knowledge.
  2. Pre-Reading: Introduce key vocabulary from the story using images and simple definitions.
  3. Reading Activity: Students read a short story silently, then answer guided questions about the main idea and details.
  4. Post-Reading Discussion: Lead a class discussion about the story's themes and clarify any confusing points.
  5. Assessment: Have students write a one-paragraph summary of the story to demonstrate comprehension.

This lesson demonstrates my approach of scaffolding activities and checking for understanding at each stage to ensure every student is engaged and learning.
