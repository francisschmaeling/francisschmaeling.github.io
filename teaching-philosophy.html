Teaching Philosophy

As an English Language Arts and Reading educator in the Dallas ISD Alternative Certification Program, I base my teaching practice on evidence-based instruction and maintaining a calm classroom culture. I have a deep love of language and strive to inspire the same passion in my students.

I blend structured literacy techniques with explicit vocabulary support for multilingual learners. My approach is warm-demanding, meaning I set high expectations for every student while providing the support they need to succeed.
